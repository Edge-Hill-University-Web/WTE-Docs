Delete a PageÂ¶

To delete a page, move your mouse over the icon and then select the Delete menu item. As deleting the page cannot be undone, you are then asked to confirm that you want to delete the part.

Warning

Deleting a part will also delete any assets attached to it. This action cannot be undone.
