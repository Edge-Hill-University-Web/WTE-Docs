Videos¶

The WTE supports including YouTube videos into the content. Simply copy the share URL from the video’s YouTube page into the .. youtube:: URL directive.

The following ReST:

.. youtube:: http://youtu.be/XXXXX

will automatically embed the YouTube video at that point ni the content.
